Introducing Fedora MiMe

Why Not fastestmirror?


Good Question
But it's awful

...fastestmirror is based on a faulty concept, when it's turned on your dnf pings all the servers in the list it has and takes the fastest ping time as the "fastestmirror" and it goes to that one as the server...
-Fedora Forum

From my personal experience, I used to get around 500 Kb/s
After measuring with it now I get around 5 Mb/s! Yeah I have slow internet

If we look into Ubuntu's Software Center or Arch's Reflector, they all measure download speed from servers
For some reason Fedora determines fastest mirror by ping time -_-
This app fixes the issue
